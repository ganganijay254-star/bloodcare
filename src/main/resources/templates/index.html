Donation basics

Useful information before you book a visit.

  • Typical donor age range is 18 to 65 years.
  • Most donors should weigh at least 50 kg.
  • You should feel healthy and well on donation day.
  • Your dashboard keeps visit records and certificates accessible.
Quick answers

Common questions

How often can I donate blood?

Most healthy adults can donate whole blood roughly every 3 months.

Is donation safe?

Yes. Hospitals use sterile, regulated procedures for safe collection.

Can I review my donation records later?

Yes. Visit history, certificates, and request updates are available from your account.
