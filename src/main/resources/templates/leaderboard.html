How Points Are Calculated 🎯

💉 Donation Points

100 points per unit of blood donated

Example: 1 unit = 100 points

🎁 Age Bonus Points

5 points per year of age (18-65 years)

Example: Age 30 = 60 points (30-18) × 5

⭐ Donation Bonus

200 points per successful blood donation

Example: 2 donations = 400 bonus points

🏆 Total Points

Sum of all above categories determines your rank

Higher points = Higher rank on leaderboard
